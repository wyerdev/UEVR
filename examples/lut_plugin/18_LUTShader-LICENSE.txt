LUTPlugin for UEVR
==================

Original shader:
  Marty's LUT shader 1.0 for ReShade 3.0
  Copyright (c) 2008-2016 Marty McFly (Pascal Gilcher)
  Source: https://github.com/crosire/reshade-shaders/blob/slim/Shaders/LUT.fx

Modifications:
  - Ported from ReShade FX to DX11/DX12 C++ plugin with embedded HLSL pixel shader.
  - LUT image (`lut.png`, 1024x32 RGBA8) loaded as an external texture via
    `EffectRuntime::replace_external_texture_png()`; selection is hot-swappable.
  - Added preset discovery across `<UEVR>/data/plugins/shader_assets/` (shipped)
    and `<persistent>/data/plugins/shader_settings/` (per-game override).

Bundled LUT images
------------------
The PNG LUT images shipped in `examples/lut_plugin/assets/` (`lut.png`,
`lut_bleach.png`, `lut_cinematic.png`, `lut_cool.png`, `lut_warm.png`) are
generated procedurally by `scripts/make_test_luts.py` (pure mathematical
transforms — identity, warm, cool, teal-and-orange, bleach-bypass) and are
distributed under the same MIT terms as the plugin wrapper.
