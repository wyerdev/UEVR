AdaptiveTonemapperPlugin for UEVR
=================================

Original shader:
  AdaptiveTonemapper.fx (luluco250 / Lucas Melo)
  Source: https://github.com/luluco250/FXShaders/blob/master/Shaders/AdaptiveTonemapper.fx
  License: MIT (per repository LICENSE,
    https://github.com/luluco250/FXShaders/blob/master/LICENSE)

Embedded ACES.fxh:
  Baking Lab — MJP and David Neubelt, fit by Stephen Hill (@self_shadow)
  Source: https://github.com/luluco250/FXShaders/blob/master/Shaders/ACES.fxh
  License: MIT (per file header)

Modifications:
  - Ported from ReShade FX to DX11/DX12 C++ plugin with embedded HLSL pixel shaders.
  - Vertex-shader interpolants `inv_white` and `exposure` were originally computed
    in MainVS and passed via TEXCOORD; recomputed inside MainPS instead because the
    UEVR effect runtime uses a fixed UV-only fullscreen vertex shader. The math is
    identical for a fullscreen triangle.
  - `ADAPTIVE_TONEMAPPER_SMALL_TEX_SIZE` and `ADAPTIVE_TONEMAPPER_SMALL_TEX_MIPLEVELS`
    are baked to their default values (256 and 9 respectively).
