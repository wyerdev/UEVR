FilmGrain2Plugin for UEVR
=========================

Original shader:
  Film Grain post-process shader v1.1
  Martins Upitis (martinsh) devlog-martinsh.blogspot.com 2013
  Uses perlin noise shader by toneburst from
  http://machinesdontcare.wordpress.com/2009/06/25/3d-perlin-noise-sphere-vertex-shader-sourcecode/
  Source: https://github.com/byxor/thug-pro-reshade/blob/master/THUG%20Pro/reshade-shaders/Shaders/FilmGrain2.fx

  License. So you are free to share, modify and adapt it for your needs,
  and even use it for commercial use.
  https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/3.0/

Modifications: Ported from ReShade FX to DX11/DX12 C++ plugin with
embedded HLSL pixel shader. Timer source changed from ReShade uniform
to GetTickCount64(). Screen size and aspect ratio passed via constant
buffer instead of ReShade built-in uniforms.
