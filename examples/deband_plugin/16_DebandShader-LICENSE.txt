DebandPlugin for UEVR
=====================

Original shader:
  Deband shader by haasn (Niklas Haas)
  Copyright (c) 2015 Niklas Haas
  Source: https://github.com/haasn/gentoo-conf/blob/xor/home/nand/.mpv/shaders/deband-pre.glsl
  License: MIT

  Modified and optimized for ReShade by JPulowski:
  Source: https://github.com/crosire/reshade-shaders/blob/slim/Shaders/Deband.fx

  Ordered dithering algorithm by CeeJay.dk.

Modifications: Ported from ReShade FX to DX11/DX12 C++ plugin with
embedded HLSL pixel shader. Depth-based filtering removed (not available
in UEVR pre-render callback).
