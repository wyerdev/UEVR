ColourfulnessPlugin for UEVR
============================

Original shader:
  Colourfulness - version 2018-11-12
  by bacondither
  EXPECTS FULL RANGE GAMMA LIGHT
  Source: https://github.com/byxor/thug-pro-reshade/blob/master/THUG%20Pro/reshade-shaders/Shaders/Colourfulness.fx

Modifications: Ported from ReShade FX to DX11/DX12 C++ plugin with
embedded HLSL pixel shader. Uses fast_luma=1 path. Temporal dither
disabled (no random uniform source in plugin context).
