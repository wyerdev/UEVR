FXAAPlugin for UEVR
===================

Original shader:
  FXAA 3.11 by CeeJayDK (ReShade wrapper) + NVIDIA FXAA 3.11 by Timothy Lottes
  ReShade source: https://github.com/CeeJayDK/SweetFX/blob/master/Shaders/SweetFX/FXAA.fx
  ReShade source: https://github.com/CeeJayDK/SweetFX/blob/master/Shaders/SweetFX/FXAA.fxh
  NVIDIA source: https://github.com/lyntel/GraphicsSamples/blob/master/samples/es3-kepler/FXAA/FXAA3_11.h
  Repository license: https://github.com/lyntel/GraphicsSamples/blob/master/license.txt

Modifications: Ported from ReShade FX to DX11/DX12 C++ plugin with
embedded HLSL pixel shaders.
